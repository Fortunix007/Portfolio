About

Precision in Setting Out & Surveying

Hello there

Hi, I'm Fortune — I am a Site Engineer and British citizen with practical knowledge in surveying, setting out, and construction control.

I specialize in resection, levelling, topographic surveys, and as-built documentation. My background combines both technical training and hands-on fieldwork, where I've completed projects such as garden foundation surveys, building corner as-builts, and levelling exercises – all documented with drawings, QA workbooks, and reports.

What sets me apart is my focus on accuracy and workflow efficiency: from creating control networks and staking coordinates to exporting data for AutoCAD and producing final reports. I am confident in reading construction drawings, interpreting gridlines, and translating them into precise site layouts. My goal is to bring precision, reliability, and efficiency to every project, ensuring that works on site are executed correctly the first time."
